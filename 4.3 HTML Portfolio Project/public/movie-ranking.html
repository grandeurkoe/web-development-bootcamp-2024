The Best Movies According to Vishal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Darlings

Darlings is a compelling movie about a woman who endures abuse from her husband. It explores how leaving an abusive marriage can seem impossible, leading some women to consider extreme measures, such as killing their husbands, as a desperate alternative.

Mimi

Mimi is a movie about a woman named Mimi who dreams of becoming a Bollywood star but lacks the financial means to move to Mumbai. To earn money, she becomes a surrogate for an American couple unable to have children. When the couple abandons her after discovering that the child has a mental disability, Mimi faces the difficult decision of giving up her dreams and embarks on a journey as a single mother.

Badhaai Do

Badhaai Do is a film about a gay man and a lesbian woman who decide to marry each other to avoid their parents' constant pressure. As you can imagine, chaos ensues.
